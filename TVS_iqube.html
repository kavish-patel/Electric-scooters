TVS IQUBE

The TVS iQube Electric is powered by a 4.4kW electric hub motor and a set of 2.25kWh li-ion batteries that propel the e-scooter to a claimed top speed of 78kmph. It can travel up to 75km on a full charge. It's quick too, bolting from 0 to 40kmph in 4.2 seconds. Although, the scooter is restricted to a top speed of 40kmph in Eco mode. In terms of underpinnings, the scooter uses a telescopic fork and twin shocks at the rear. The scooter is anchored by a front disc and a drum unit at the rear. Its 12-inch wheels have been borrowed from the Jupiter Grande. The iQube weighs in at 118kg (kerb).

iQube Electric can go upto 75 km/charge after a full charge.

iQube Electric battery takes 6 Hours to get charged.

The TVS iQube Electric boasts a lot of features, including a full-colour digital display that connects to your smartphone, LED lights, an illuminating logo near the drivetrain, an underseat light, and a USB charging socket. Other notable features include Q-park assist, riding modes, day and night display, and regenerative braking. Thanks to smartphone connectivity, you can check past ride stats, overspeeding and emergency alerts, battery charge status, last park location, as well as get navigation assist and incoming call/SMS alerts.

The TVS iQube electric scooter will go up against the Ather 450 Plus and Bajaj Chetak. TVS is offering a 3-year/50,000 km warranty on the iQube.
